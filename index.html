Resourcify Global Solutions specializes in two core service areas: Full-Cycle Recruitment for UK-based organizations and Medical Billing & Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) for US healthcare providers. Our medical billing services encompass insurance verification, coding, claims submission, denial management, and accounts receivable optimization.
